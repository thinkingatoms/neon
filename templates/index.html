    About Us

    ThinkingAtoms started when I, Tom Huang, was trying to compare my own portfolio against certain sections of S&P 1500, and couldn't find a free easy way of doing it.

    I used to work on benchmark construction and performance for six years at one of the largest asset management companies, and decided to roll my own trade performance and daily attribution calculators from scratch using publicly available pricing sources.

    This site is a byproduct that I hope others may benefit from, and would love to hear your comments and suggestions!
